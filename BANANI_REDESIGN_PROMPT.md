- **Fade in up**: Text elements (0.6s duration)
- **Scale in**: Cards and badges (0.5s, spring effect)

- **Cards**: Lift up (-8px), shadow glow, scale 1.02
- **Buttons**: Scale 1.05, glow shadow

- **Gradient mesh**: Radial gradients with low opacity
- **Rotating circles**: Slow 50-60s rotation, blur effects
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur on cards, transparency
- **Pulse animations**: Soft pulsing on badges and icons

### Scroll Animations:
- **Fade in on viewport**: Elements animate when scrolling into view
- **Progress indicators**: Animated dots, arrows, progress bars
